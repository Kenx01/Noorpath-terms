Privacy Policy

NoorPath (“we”, “us”, “our”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how you can manage it.

1. What We Collect

  • Account Information: Name, email, and registration details.
  • Learning Data: Course progress, quiz scores, and user preferences.

2. How We Use It

  • To provide and improve NoorPath learning experiences.
  • To personalize your journey and enhance your user experience.

3. How We Protect Your Data

  • Data is stored securely with modern encryption and access control standards.
  • We never sell or rent your personal information to third parties.

4. Sharing and Third Parties

  • We may share data with trusted service providers (e.g., analytics or hosting) strictly for NoorPath-related services.

5. Your Rights

  • You can request to view, correct, or delete your data at any time.

6. Cookies & Analytics

  • We use cookies to improve app performance and user experience. You may disable them through browser settings.

7. Changes to this Policy

  • We may update this policy to stay compliant with regulations. Updates will be posted on this page.
